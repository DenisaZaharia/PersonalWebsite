Overview

Wishing to provide people with better socialising options post COVID-19, 4 students from Fontys University of Applied sciences teamed up, and created an unique concept, that focuses on connecting people based on shared interests, not taking into account physical aspect.

Why connectYall?

Due to lockdowns and restrictions, people became more isolated and many previously common activities started to feel out of place, even developing social anxiety for some people. For international students like most of us, socialising and finding friends with the same interests as us was even harder, as online education was still happening and social activities were not yet organised. connectYall comes in help to those people who wish to bond over mutual interests, without the pressure of looking good at all times or useless small conversation - you can skip directly to what you enjoy doing!

Branding

It’s always tough coming up with a name for a website, there aren’t many domain names left so we used a great little tool called Lean Domain Search to come up with a suitable name with an available domain. The name needed to be short, easy to say and spell, and also needed to convey what the actual website was for. After throwing a bunch of ideas around, we combined the words “connect” and “everyone” to come up with “ConnectYall"

Sketching the interface

After doing a quick round of guerilla research, we were able to define if our difficulties with socialisation post COVID-19 were the case for other people too, and if so to identify a target audience for which we were able to identify their goals and needs. After finalizing an idea, our next step was to create wireframes, as having a design representation in our mind would not help us that much.

Prototype & Color Pallete

We started our visualisation for our product by choosing a colour palette. Wanting to induce a feeling of friendliness and joy to our users, as well as the feeling of safety and calmness, we came up with this colour palette for our application and our promotional products.
